I’m an Applied Scientist at Amazon, building and optimizing large language models for real-world applications. Previously, I worked on LLMs to enhance the shopping experience in the Stores Foundational AI team, where I trained small to mid-sized models and developed them into scalable production systems. More recently, on the Prime Video Search team, my work focuses on real-time query understanding for agentic search using medium-sized language models, with a strong emphasis on latency optimization and efficient inference.

Before joining Amazon, I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) in 2022, where I was advised by Alice Oh. During my Ph.D., I interned as an Applied Scientist at Amazon and as a Research Scientist at Adobe.
